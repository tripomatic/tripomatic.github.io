title: Planning a trip on the website
---

Welcome to [Tripomatic](https://maps.tripomatic.com). The website allows you to discover places around the world and create travel itineraries.

## Creating a Trip

1. Go to [maps.tripomatic.com](https://maps.tripomatic.com)
2. Click on **Trip** in the top menu
3. Click **Create new trip**
4. Describe your trip - you can type naturally like "5 days in Paris next month"
5. The AI will detect your destination and suggest dates
6. Click **Create trip** to start planning

## Working with the Map

The map shows places you might want to visit. Click any marker to see details about a place.

**Filter places by category:**
- Click the search bar to see category filters
- Select a category to show only those places on the map

**Add a place to your trip:**
1. Click a place on the map
2. Click **Add to trip**
3. Choose which day to add it to

**Save a place for later:**
- Click the heart icon to add it to your Shortlist
- Or click **Favorite** to add it to your permanent favorites

## Using the Shortlist

The Shortlist is for places you're considering but haven't added to your itinerary yet.

1. Click **Trip** in the navigation
2. Scroll down to see your Shortlist
3. Drag places from the Shortlist to a day when you're ready

## Custom Places

To create a custom place:

1. Click anywhere on the map, or search for an address or GPS coordinates (e.g., 49.232883, 16.572891)
2. Click **Add to trip** or **Add to Shortlist**
3. Edit the name and details as needed

## Itinerary

Switch between days using the day tabs in your trip panel. Each day shows:
- The places you've added
- Routes between places
- Estimated travel times

Click on a transit segment to change the transport mode (walking, driving, bike, public transit, etc.).

## Save & Export

Sign in to save your trip and sync it across devices. Premium subscribers can:
- **Download a PDF guide** of your trip
- **Export to GPX** for navigation devices
- **Export to KML** for Google Earth

Click on trip settings (gear icon) to access export options.

## Download the Mobile App

Get the Tripomatic app for [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/tripomatic/id519058033) or [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tripomatic). Your trips sync automatically when you sign in.
