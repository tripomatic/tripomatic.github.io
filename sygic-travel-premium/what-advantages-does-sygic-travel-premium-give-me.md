---
title: What advantages does Tripomatic Premium give me?
---

Tripomatic Premium unlocks the full potential of Tripomatic on all devices as long as you are signed in with the same account.

**Premium Features**

- **Trip planner with unlimited trips** - Create and manage as many trips as you want
- **AI assistant and AI-powered research** - Get personalized recommendations, automatic itinerary suggestions, and smart trip planning powered by AI
- **Outdoor, winter, and satellite maps** - Access premium map layers for hiking, skiing, and detailed satellite imagery
- **Export trips** - Download your trips as PDF, GPX (for navigation), or KML (for Google Earth)

By subscribing to Tripomatic Premium, you also support the development of more advanced features to come. Thank you for that! We appreciate it.
