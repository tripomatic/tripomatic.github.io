---
title: Can I import files into Tripomatic?
---

Importing trip files (GPX, KML, etc.) into Tripomatic is not currently supported.

## Export Options

While you can't import files, Premium subscribers can export their trips in several formats:

- **PDF** - A printable guide with maps and details
- **GPX** - For GPS devices and navigation apps
- **KML** - For Google Earth and other mapping tools

See [Downloading the PDF guide]({% link on-the-road/downloading-the-pdf-guide.md %}) for export instructions.
