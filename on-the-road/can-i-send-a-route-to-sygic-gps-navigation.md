---
title: Can I send a route from Tripomatic to Sygic GPS Navigation?
---

Yes! If you have Sygic GPS Navigation installed on your mobile device, Tripomatic can open routes directly in the app.

## How to Navigate with Sygic

1. Open any place in Tripomatic
2. Tap **Navigate** or **Directions**
3. Tap **Open in map app**
4. Select **Sygic** from the list of available apps
5. Sygic opens with the destination ready to navigate

## Navigating Your Itinerary

To navigate to the next place in your itinerary:

1. Open your trip and select the current day
2. Tap on the place you want to navigate to
3. Tap **Navigate** and choose Sygic

The route opens directly in Sygic GPS Navigation where you can start turn-by-turn navigation.
