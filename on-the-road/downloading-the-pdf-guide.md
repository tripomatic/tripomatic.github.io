---
title: Downloading the PDF guide
---

Premium subscribers can export their trips as PDF guides to use offline or print.

## How to Export

**On mobile:**
1. Open your trip
2. Tap **Trip** in the navigation
3. Scroll down and tap **Export**
4. Select **PDF**
5. Tap **Preview** to view or **Download** to save

**On web:**
1. Open your trip at [maps.tripomatic.com](https://maps.tripomatic.com)
2. Click the settings icon next to your trip name
3. Select **Export**
4. Choose **PDF** and download

## What's Included

The PDF guide contains:
- Trip overview with dates and destinations
- Day-by-day itinerary with maps
- Place details including addresses and notes
- Accommodation information
- Your custom notes

## Other Export Formats

You can also export your trip as:
- **GPX** - For GPS devices and navigation apps
- **KML** - For Google Earth and other mapping tools

Export requires Tripomatic Premium.
