---
title: What Is the AI Assistant and How Do I Use It?
---

The AI assistant in Tripomatic 26 helps you plan your trips with intelligent suggestions and answers to your travel questions.

### What Can the AI Assistant Do?

- Help you discover places to visit
- Suggest activities based on your interests
- Answer questions about your destinations
- Help organize your itinerary

### Subscription Required

Access to the AI assistant is available with an active **Tripomatic Premium yearly subscription**, which includes all premium features.

If you purchased a lifetime Premium version before Tripomatic 26, it still gives you access to non-AI premium features. To use the AI features, you need to subscribe to the **AI Upgrade**.
