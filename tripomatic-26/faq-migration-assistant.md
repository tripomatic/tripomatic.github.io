---
title: How Do I Access the Migration Assistant?
---

The Migration Assistant lets you transfer data from the previous version of Tripomatic to Tripomatic 26.

### How to Access It

1. Open Tripomatic 26
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Migration Assistant**

### What You Can Do

From the Migration Assistant, you can:

- **Migrate my account** - Transfer everything (settings, favorites, and all trips)
- **Migrate Favorites** - Transfer only your favorites
- **Migrate New Trips** - Transfer only trips that haven't been transferred yet
- **Transfer individual trips** - Select specific trips to transfer one by one
