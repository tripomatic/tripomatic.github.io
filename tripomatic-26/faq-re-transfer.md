---
title: What If I Made Changes in the Old App After Transferring?
---

You can re-transfer individual trips using the Migration Assistant.

### How to Re-transfer a Trip

1. Open Tripomatic 26
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Migration Assistant**
4. Find the trip you want to re-transfer
5. Tap the download icon next to it

The existing trip in Tripomatic 26 will be replaced with the updated version from the old app.
